Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Aysen Zaptcioglu and Can Zaptcioglu ("we", "our", or "us") operate under the legal entity AYSEN ZAPTCIOGLU and our applications, including Flux: Subscriptions Tracker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our Apple iOS applications.

1. Information Collection and Use

We build our apps with privacy in mind. For most of our applications, specifically Flux: Subscriptions Tracker, we do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). All your app data, such as subscription details and settings, is stored locally on your device or securely in your personal Apple iCloud account.

2. Apple Standard Privacy Compliance

In accordance with Apple's App Store Review Guidelines, we ensure that:

  • We only request permissions that are absolutely necessary for the app to function.
  • Any data handled by our apps remains strictly on your device or in your personal iCloud.
  • We do not use third-party tracking or analytics SDKs that covertly collect user data.

3. Third-party Services

Our applications do not integrate with third-party services that extract your personal data. The apps communicate exclusively with Apple's secure services (such as iCloud) to sync your data across your own devices.

4. Security

We value your trust in using our applications. Because we do not transmit your local data to our own servers, the security of your information relies primarily on Apple's device-level protections and iCloud encryption. Please ensure your device is secured with a passcode, Touch ID, or Face ID.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at zaptcioglucan@gmail.com.
